Hello my name is Orlando Cabalo and I am a second year History Major at the University of California Riverside. I am also an elected official for the Associated Students of the University of California Riverside. I strongly believe that from every business to every government agency, there is a need for skilled and determined individuals who will not stop until the job is done. I believe I am one of those individuals because of my work ethich, my desire to exceed in what I do, and my tenacity in everything I do.

Starting with my academic experience, I'd say that I have a proven track record of doing what it takes to succeed. From long study periods to always trying to set myself apart, I have been able to excell academically. In high school I graduated with honors (3.54GPA) and in university I have been able to maintain a 3.8 GPA. I have an intense interest for the humanities, from politics to history, I love to learn the tools of the present & past. I think that studying the past and studying the inner-workings of government will allow myself to help others in these different sectors. After graduating in 2023, I hope to attend a law school and practice law. Additionally, on the side, I'd like to take up a second job/hobby to help others with food insecurity and political engagement.

In terms of my prvious work experience, it can be primarily analyzed in two sectors; local government and the service industry. In the service industry, I have worked as both a Courtesy Clerk at Stater Brothers' Grocery Store and as a Guest Service Associate at Knott's Berry Farm. In these roles I honed my physical abilities to carry out my job and my customer service. I found it is critical to have a good countenance and a pep in your voice when helping customers, since it helps them feel more at home. Recently, I have transalated the information I've learned in the service industry into political office. As the President Pro Tempore of the Associated Students of UC Riverside, I have strived myself on helping other students. Primarily I have been at work reforming the local government and diverting more resources to student basic needs.
